Description

Dubai is a world-famous shopping hub. On this tour, you will be guided to the top shopping destinations beginning with a stop at ‘City Centre’, one of the biggest malls in Deira Dubai. after which you will proceed to Bur Dubai for a short walk through ‘Bur Juman’ shopping center. After visiting both the malls you will move to explore the bargains at the Gold Souk (market) and savor the aroma of exotic spices while strolling through the spice souk. Gold Souk is packed with hundreds of gold shops and other shops with some unique souvenirs at the old-fashioned yet full-of-life especially Meena Bazaar. At the same time, you will explore Dubai’s multi-ethnic cuisines by making your way to the irresistible Spice Souk.
