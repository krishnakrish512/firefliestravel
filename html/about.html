FIREFLIES TRAVEL & TOURS PVT. LTD. is a leading professional travel agency specializing in outbound tours. We are a trustworthy and reliable company combining years of experience in the travel industry with a deep understanding of the foreign culture. We excel in creating various outbound tour packages for our customers so that they may experience the rich history, local culture and breathtaking landscapes of foreign lands. Our mission is to provide high-quality services at reasonable prices for both novice and seasoned Nepalese travelers.

All our outbound tours are fully flexible and can be customized to meet the interests of the travelers. Our knowledgeable and well-trained staff will help you create the best custom travel packages at the most affordable cost. Whether you are planning your first trip to any foreign countries, our rate is competitive and our travel options are diverse. Even before your journey begins, you will enjoy our high-quality personal service while creating your itinerary and planning your trip. We are well equipped with a secure online booking system supplemented by professional reservation agents who will take care to address any travel concerns you may have before your trip begins. Upon your arrival in any of the outbound countries, our local company representatives will greet you at the airport and escort you to your hotel. During your entire tour our highly experienced, professional, multilingual tour guides will be readily available to ensure your safety, comfort, and enjoyment.

Let your experience of outbound tours begin and have your dream vacation come true with FIREFLIES TRAVEL & TOURS PVT. LTD. All along the way, we will be at your service.
